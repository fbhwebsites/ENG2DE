Mental Health in Youth

A public health crisis since the Covid 19 pandemic.

psychology 3 in 4
Youths reported a worsened mental health during the pandemic.
Did you know?
Early support can make a huge difference in a young person's life. You're not alone!
trending_up 50%
of mental health disorders emerge before 15 years of age.
Did you know?
This statistic rises to 75% by the age of 25.
security Vulnerability
Youths are more susceptible to psychological distress and mental illness
Why?
Youths are still developing their brains and emotional regulation. Disruptions to this development leads to decreased resilience and increased vulnerability to mental health issues.
